Privacy Policy

By using this website, you agree to the terms outlined below. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • When you contact us via info@vavavuum.com, we collect your name, email address, and any details you include in your message.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you browse Vavavuum.com, we may automatically collect:

We use the data collected to:
