Top Nightlife Hotels in Las Vegas

The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas

Average Price Per Night:$250–$350

  • Home to Marquee Nightclub and Dayclub, one of Vegas’ top nightlife venues.

  • Rooftop pool parties with private cabanas and bottle service.

  • Stylish suites with private terraces and panoramic Strip views.

The Cosmopolitan is one of the best nightlife hotels in Vegas, offering a high-energy mix of gaming, dining, and entertainment. Marquee Nightclub is a Vegas icon, drawing top DJs and a social crowd. The Dayclub keeps the party going in the sun, while the casino and bars offer a nonstop atmosphere.

Wynn Las Vegas

Average Price Per Night:$300–$400

  • XS Nightclub delivers world-class DJs, high-end bottle service, and a luxury club vibe.

  • Encore Beach Club is a top-tier day party spot with private bungalows and VIP service

  • Upscale gaming, fine dining, and premium spa services add to the experience.

Wynn Las Vegas is a prime choice for travelers who want luxury alongside a nightlife scene. XS Nightclub is one of the most exclusive in Vegas, attracting A-list performers and high-end guests. MGM Grand

Average Price Per Night:$150–$250

  • Hakkasan Nightclub is a massive multi-level club featuring top DJs and an energetic dance floor.

  • Wet Republic Ultra Pool offers a high-energy day party scene.

  • Large casino floor with a sportsbook, slots, and table games.

MGM Grand is one of the best nightlife hotels in Las Vegas, known for its club scene and entertainment. Hakkasan is a world-famous club with an electric atmosphere, while Wet Republic keeps the energy high during the day. The casino and live shows make MGM a go-to for those who want action around the clock.

ARIA is one of the top Strip hotels with nightlife, offering both an upscale gaming scene and an exclusive club atmosphere. Jewel Nightclub features cutting-edge lighting and sound design, while the resort’s cocktail bars provide a sophisticated pre-game or late-night setting.

Caesars Palace is a must-visit for those looking for top-tier nightlife in Las Vegas. OMNIA Nightclub delivers a high-energy experience with its world-famous DJ lineup and stylish atmosphere. The casino and pool areas provide plenty of options to enjoy before and after a night out.
