Paris Las Vegas Hotel & Casino

Average Price Per Night: $150–$250

  • Romantic ambiance with replicas of Parisian landmarks.

  • Multiple dining venues including French cuisine.

  • Leisure facilities such as a rooftop pool and spa.

Bellagio Las Vegas

Average Price Per Night: $250–$400

  • High-end casino with poker rooms, table games, and luxury slots.

  • Prime Strip location near nightlife and entertainment.

MGM Grand

Average Price Per Night: $150–$250

  • Home to Hakkasan Nightclub, a must-visit for top DJs and high-energy crowds.
