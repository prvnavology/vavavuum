Las Vegas Party Hotels

Non-Stop Action, Pool Parties & VIP Nightlife

Las Vegas is the ultimate destination for high-energy stays, and party hotels are where the fun never stops. Think rooftop pools, world-class DJs, and legendary nightclubs—all within steps of your suite. Top Party Hotels in Las Vegas

MGM Grand

Average Price Per Night:$150–$250

  • Home to Hakkasan Nightclub, a must-visit for top DJs and high-energy crowds.

  • Wet Republic Ultra Pool keeps the party going all day.

  • Prime location on the Las Vegas Strip for easy access to clubs and casinos.

MGM Grand is built for party lovers. Hakkasan Nightclub is a Vegas staple, delivering electrifying performances from world-famous DJs. Wet Republic Ultra Pool is where the action is during the day, with VIP cabanas, bottle service, and poolside beats. If you want non-stop excitement from check-in to checkout, MGM Grand delivers.

Caesars Palace

Average Price Per Night:$200–$300

  • Garden of the Gods Pool Oasis is the place for stylish daytime lounging.

  • Located in the heart of the Strip, surrounded by top nightlife.

Caesars Palace is where classic Vegas meets next-level nightlife. OMNIA Nightclub is a spectacle, featuring stunning visuals and performances from the best EDM artists. The Garden of the Gods Pool Oasis keeps the energy high with multiple pools and luxury cabanas. If you want a mix of luxury and high-energy nightlife, Caesars Palace is a perfect fit.

  • Central Strip location puts you right in the action.

The Venetian Resort is all about luxury meets party. TAO Nightclub is an iconic Vegas venue, known for its intimate yet wild atmosphere. By day, TAO Beach Dayclub offers poolside beats and bottle service under the sun. If you want to party in style, The Venetian has it all.

Wynn Las Vegas is where luxury meets non-stop entertainment. Encore Beach Club throws some of the biggest pool parties in town, featuring world-class DJs and an elite crowd.
