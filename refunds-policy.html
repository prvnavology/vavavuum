Refunds Policy

At Vavavuum, we provide information on Las Vegas hotels but do not process bookings, payments, or refunds. This Refunds Policy outlines how refunds are handled and what to do if you need assistance with a reservation.

By using Vavavuum.com, you acknowledge that all hotel bookings are subject to the policies of the third-party providers we link to.

1. Vavavuum Does Not Process Hotel Bookings or Payments

Vavavuum is an informational platform that helps users find the best Las Vegas hotels, including party hotels, casino resorts, nightlife hotels, and all-inclusive stays.

  • We do not accept payments for hotel reservations.

  • We do not issue refunds, as we do not handle bookings directly.

  • We do not have control over cancellation or refund policies set by third-party booking platforms or hotels.

If you have booked a hotel through a third-party website linked from Vavavuum, you must contact that provider directly for any refund inquiries.

2. How to Request a Refund

If you need a refund for a hotel reservation, follow these steps:

  • Check the Refund Policy of the Booking Site Before booking, always review the cancellation and refund terms provided by the hotel or third-party booking platform. Some hotels offer free cancellations, while others may have non-refundable rates.

  • Contact the Hotel or Booking Provider Directly Since all reservations are made through external platforms, you must reach out to the hotel or the booking site where you completed your reservation. Common booking providers include:

  • Official hotel websites

  • Online travel agencies (OTAs) like Expedia, Booking.com, Hotels.com

  • Casino resort websites

  • Review Payment Dispute Options If you experience issues with an unauthorized charge, incorrect billing, or a refund delay, consider:

  • Disputing the charge with your credit card provider or payment processor.

4. Non-Refundable & Special Rate Bookings

Some hotels and booking providers offer non-refundable or discounted rates with specific conditions. These may include:

  • Prepaid Non-Refundable Rates: Certain bookings require full prepayment and cannot be refunded.

  • Last-Minute Deals: Some discounted reservations may not allow changes or cancellations.

  • Special Event & Holiday Rates: Bookings during peak Las Vegas events (such as conventions, concerts, or sports events) may have strict refund policies.

It is the responsibility of the traveler to verify the terms before booking.
