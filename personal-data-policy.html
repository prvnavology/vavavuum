Personal Data Policy

At Vavavuum, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Personal Data Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with Vavavuum.com.

By using this website, you consent to the data practices described below. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use immediately.

1. What Personal Data We Collect

When you visit Vavavuum.com, we may collect the following types of personal data:

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Form Submissions: When you contact us through our website, we collect your name, email address, and any details included in your message.

  • Newsletter Subscriptions: If you subscribe to receive updates, we collect your email address.

  • Email Correspondence: Any information you voluntarily share when reaching out to info@vavavuum.com.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you browse Vavavuum, certain information is collected automatically:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs.

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the site, and interaction with content.

  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: See Section 5: Cookies & Tracking for details.

1.3 Third-Party Data

  • When you interact with third-party links (such as hotel booking sites), they may collect additional data.

  • We may receive anonymized data from analytics providers to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services - Ensure website functionality, enhance user experience, and optimize performance.

  • To Communicate with You - Respond to inquiries, send requested updates, and provide relevant information.

  • To Analyze Site Performance - Monitor traffic patterns and measure engagement.

  • To Prevent Fraud & Ensure Security - Detect suspicious activity and protect against cyber threats.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. How We Share Your Personal Data

We only share personal data in limited circumstances:

3.1 With Trusted Third-Party Service Providers

We use reputable service providers to assist in operating our site, such as:

  • Email Marketing Platforms - To manage newsletter subscriptions.

  • Analytics Providers - To track user behavior and improve website performance.

These providers operate under strict confidentiality agreements and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

We may disclose personal data if required by law or to:

Our website contains links to third-party sites, such as Las Vegas hotel booking platforms. If you click on an external link, you will be subject to that site’s privacy policy. Vavavuum is not responsible for third-party data handling.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize returning visitors, save preferences, and analyze site performance.

5.3 Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. However, doing so may affect site performance.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@vavavuum.com.
