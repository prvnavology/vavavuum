How to Get VIP Access to the Best Nightclubs

Las Vegas is home to some of the most exclusive nightclubs in the world. Getting past the long lines and into VIP areas requires strategy, planning, and sometimes, a little investment.

Get on the Guest List

Many Las Vegas clubs offer guest lists for free or discounted entry.

  • Check club websites and promoters – Clubs like Hakkasan, XS, and Marquee have guest lists available online.

  • Arrive early – Guest list entry typically closes by 11 PM, so don’t be late.

  • Dress to impress – Strict dress codes apply. No sneakers, shorts, or casual wear.

Table Service & Bottle Packages

If you want the full VIP experience, reserving table service is the way to go.

  • Book in advance – Popular clubs sell out their best tables early

  • Minimum spend applies – Expect a minimum spend requirement that varies by club and event.

  • Split costs with a group – If you’re with a group, pooling funds for a table can be more cost-effective than buying individual drinks.

Pro Tips for VIP Access

  • Tip the right people – A generous tip to the doorman or host can work wonders.

  • Be social with promoters – Networking with club promoters can get you access to VIP sections.

  • Know the big nights – Fridays and Saturdays are peak nights. Midweek visits can mean easier VIP access.

With the right planning, you can skip the long lines and party like a Vegas VIP.
